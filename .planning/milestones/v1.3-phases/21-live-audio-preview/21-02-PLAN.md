  <action>
Human verification of live audio preview. The useAudioMix hook and PlaybackTimeline integration were completed in Plan 01. This checkpoint verifies audible output in a real browser.

What was built:
Web Audio API integration that mixes audio from unmuted video cells during editor playback. The useAudioMix hook creates an AudioContext on first play, wires GainNodes per video cell, normalizes volume to 1/N active cells, and reacts to mute toggles in real time.

How to verify:
1. Open the app at http://localhost:5173
2. Drop a video file WITH audio into a cell
3. Press play — confirm you hear audio from the video through your speakers (LAUD-01)
4. Press pause — confirm audio stops immediately (LAUD-02)
5. Press play again, then click the mute toggle for the cell — confirm audio goes silent while video continues playing (LAUD-03)
6. Unmute the cell — confirm audio returns (LAUD-03, LAUD-05 reactivity)
7. Drop a second video with audio into another cell — press play — confirm both videos produce audio at roughly equal volume (LAUD-01, D-04 normalized gain)
8. Mute one cell — confirm the other cell's audio gets louder (gain goes from 1/2 to 1/1) (LAUD-03, D-04)
9. Drop a video WITHOUT audio — confirm the mute icon is grayed out/locked and no audio comes from that cell (LAUD-03, from Phase 19)
10. Rapidly click play/pause 10+ times — check browser DevTools console for any InvalidStateError or other errors (LAUD-05)
11. Let playback run to the end of the longest video — confirm audio stops when timeline reaches end (LAUD-02)

Resume signal: Type "approved" if all checks pass, or describe any issues found.
