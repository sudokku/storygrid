<objective>
Add locked, non-interactive VolumeX icon rendering for no-audio video cells in both the ActionBar (portal overlay) and SelectedCellPanel (sidebar). The locked state is visually distinct from the user-muted state.

Purpose: Users see at a glance that a video has no audio track -- the toggle is grayed out and non-interactive, preventing confusion about why muting/unmuting has no effect.

Output: Two modified UI components with locked-state rendering, two updated test files.
