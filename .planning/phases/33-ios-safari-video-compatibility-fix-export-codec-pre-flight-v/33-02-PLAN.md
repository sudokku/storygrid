<objective>
Fix video first-frame reliability on iOS Safari (D-03) in both LeafNode.tsx and captureVideoThumbnail.

Purpose: iOS Safari does not fire the `seeked` event after `video.currentTime = 0` unless the video decoder has been activated via a `play()` call. Without this, video cells show a blank frame on iOS. Additionally, `captureVideoThumbnail` sets `video.src` without calling `video.load()`, which may prevent `loadedmetadata` from firing on iOS Safari.

Output: gridStore.ts with `video.load()` after src assignment and `play().catch()` in onMeta; LeafNode.tsx with `play().catch()` in onLoadedMetadata; one new test verifying both calls.
</objective>
